The Best Movies Acording to Amy

My top 3 movies off all-time

The Greatest Showman

I love this movie, it's about innovation and how to build a good team, coupled with a story of family love and never losing sight of what you really want.

Confessions of a Shopaholic

It is a film about fashion, debt, finance and love. I like this movie too much because it mixes examples of finance with fashion, and these are two topics that are of interest to me, coupled with a romantic story.

The choice

It's a movie about decisions and taking risks when it comes to love, it motivates you to take certain risks when love is worth it.
